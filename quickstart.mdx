## Setting Up Your First Monitor

Get your website monitoring up and running quickly with UptimeGuard.

### Create Your Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Sign Up for UptimeGuard">
    Visit [UptimeGuard](https://app.uptimeguard.xyz/register) to create your account. 
    The signup process only takes a minute - simply provide your email address and create a password.
    You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account before proceeding.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="gauge-high" title="Access Your Dashboard">
    After confirming your email, log in to access your [UptimeGuard Dashboard](https://app.uptimeguard.xyz/dashboard).
    This is your command center for all monitoring activities, where you'll be able to see the status of all your websites at a glance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Configure Your First Monitor

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Add a Website">
    From your dashboard, click the "Add Website" button to begin monitoring a new site.
    Enter the URL of the website you want to monitor, such as `https://example.com`.
    
    <Note>
      Make sure to include the protocol (http:// or https://) when entering your website URL.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="Configure Monitoring Settings">
    Customize your monitoring settings:
    
    1. **Monitoring Interval** - Choose how frequently UptimeGuard should check your website (e.g., every 1, 5, or 15 minutes)
    2. **Alert Settings** - Set up notification preferences for when your site goes down
    3. **Response Validation** - Optionally configure specific status codes or content checks
    
    Once configured, click "Save" to start monitoring.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Set Up Notifications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bell" title="Configure Alert Channels">
    UptimeGuard can notify you through multiple channels when issues are detected:
    
    1. Go to "Notification Settings" in your account
    2. Add and verify communication channels such as:
       - Email notifications
       - SMS alerts
       - Webhook integrations
       - Slack notifications
    
    You can customize which alerts go to which channels, and set up escalation policies for critical websites.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="mobile" title="Install Mobile App">
    Download the UptimeGuard mobile app to receive push notifications and check your website status on the go:
    
    - [iOS App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/uptimeguard)
    - [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=xyz.uptimeguard.app)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
